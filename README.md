HonorApps Privacy Policy
Effective Date: October 30, 2025
HonorApps develops and publishes various mobile applications (including music apps, drum pads, sound tools, game utilities, productivity apps, and entertainment apps). This Privacy Policy applies to all applications released by HonorApps, unless a specific app contains additional, app-specific notices within its description.
This page informs users regarding our policies on the collection, use, and disclosure of information across all HonorApps applications.

Information Collection and Use
HonorApps values user privacy and does not collect, store, or share personally identifiable information in any of our applications.
Some apps may request certain device permissions strictly for functional purposes, such as:


Storage Access: To read or save user-created audio files


Microphone Access: For recording audio (only when user chooses to record)


Network Access: For displaying ads
